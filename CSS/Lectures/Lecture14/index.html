BURGER

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese

Recipe

  1. Cook the burger patty until golden brown.
  2. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  3. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, and patty.
  4. Spread sauces and serve hot.

PIZZA

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Olives

Recipe

  1. Roll out the pizza dough on a tray.
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  3. Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes and serve hot.

PASTA

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese

Recipe

  1. Boil the pasta until soft.
  2. Cook vegetables and sauce in a pan.
  3. Mix boiled pasta with the sauce.
  4. Add cheese and serve warm.

BIRYANI

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices

Recipe

  1. Cook basmati rice until half done.
  2. Prepare spicy chicken or vegetable curry.
  3. Layer rice and curry in a pot.
  4. Cook on low flame and serve hot.
